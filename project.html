CareerLens

(HTML/css/JavaScriipt/python)


CareerLens is an innovative web application designed to assist users in identifying their ideal career paths based on their unique skills, interests, and personality traits. By leveraging advanced algorithms and a user-friendly interface, CareerLens provides personalized career recommendations, resources for skill development, and insights into various industries. Whether you're a student exploring options or a professional considering a career change, CareerLens aims to empower you with the information and tools needed to make informed career decisions.
